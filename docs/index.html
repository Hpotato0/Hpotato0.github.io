About

I am a first-year Electrical and Computer Engineering PhD candidate at Georgia Tech Computer-Aided Design Lab (GTCAD), advised by Prof. Sung-Kyu Lim. I am currently interested in computer architecture and the co-optimization of system and technology.

I received my BS from Seoul National University, where I was advised by Prof. Jung Ho Ahn on accelerating Fully Homomorphic Encryption, and by Prof. Jinho Lee on designing a DRAM function-in-memory for efficient graph processing.
